# The ZEAL Signature Hoodie - Design Specifications

## Product Overview

**Name:** The ZEAL Hoodie  
**Price:** R1,299  
**Tagline:** "The First And Only"

## Design Elements

### 1. FRONT - The Sigil
**Location:** Center chest, large  
**Design:** Large ZEAL occult symbol  
- Circular border with geometric patterns
- Central triangle formation
- Inner eye symbol
- Gold (#a08040) color
- Size: ~30cm diameter

**Visual Impact:** Bold, commanding presence that immediately identifies the wearer as part of the ZEAL circle.

---

### 2. BACK - The Full Seal
**Location:** Full back, centered  
**Design:** Expanded ZEAL sigil with text  
- Larger circular border with multiple rings
- Triangle within triangle
- All-seeing eye in center
- "ZEAL" text at bottom
- Gold (#a08040) color
- Size: ~40cm height

**Visual Impact:** The statement piece. When walking away, people see the full occult seal.

---

### 3. RIGHT SLEEVE - "As above"
**Location:** Outside of right arm, running vertically  
**Text:** "As above"  
**Font:** Cinzel, uppercase  
**Color:** Gold (#a08040)  
**Position:** Upper arm to forearm  

**Meaning:** Represents the celestial, the macrocosm, the divine principle above.

---

### 4. LEFT SLEEVE - "So below"
**Location:** Outside of left arm, running vertically  
**Text:** "So below"  
**Font:** Cinzel, uppercase  
**Color:** Gold (#a08040)  
**Position:** Upper arm to forearm  

**Meaning:** Represents the earthly, the microcosm, the reflection of the divine in the material.

**Together:** The full Hermetic principle "As above, so below" - the fundamental law of correspondence.

---

### 5. HOOD INTERIOR - The Hidden Eye
**Location:** Inside back of hood  
**Design:** Eye of Zeal  
- Large eye symbol
- Radiating lines (sunburst)
- Detailed iris and pupil
- Gold (#a08040) with black pupil
- Size: ~20cm diameter

**The Secret:** Only visible when the hood is worn. The wearer knows it's there, watching from within. A private symbol of awareness and consciousness.

**Meaning:** The eye that sees from within. Awareness that comes from looking inward.

## Manufacturing Notes

- All prints should use high-quality screen printing
- Gold ink should be metallic/shimmer effect if possible
- Hood interior eye needs to be visible but not obtrusive
- Sleeve text should be durable for washing
- Double-stitch all high-stress areas

---

## For Production

When sending to manufacturers, specify:
1. Exact placement measurements for each element
2. Pantone color for gold (closest to #a08040)
3. Print method: Screen print recommended
4. Hood interior print: Must be visible against fabric color
5. Quality: Premium streetwear standard

This is the flagship product. Quality must be exceptional.
